How we Collect E-Waste
 Electronic waste (E-waste): Describes electronic products that are unwanted, not working, nearing or at the end of their useful life cycle.

We offer a few ways to recycle your e-waste with us. You can drop off items during ANY and ALL of our events, we also offer monthly and bi-monthly pickups. We will have a dedicated drop off location in the future.

E-Waste we collect: ALL COMPUTERS, servers, motherboards, RAM, CPUs, flatscreen monitors, keyboards, mice, webcams, computer speakers, DVD/VHS players, radios, cell phones, walkmen/cd players, tablets, software, video game consoles, full/empty toner & ink cartridges.

If what you want to recycle isn't listed here please email us: service@ewastegang.org

E-Waste we DO NOT collect for free: Tube/CRT Monitors, TV's, batteries. Please contact us for more information.

Donations

We accept many forms of donations: Cash, Money Order, Check, Crypto and in-kind donations.

Crypto donations: If you would like a receipt for tax purposes, please send us your email or mailing address along with your public key and TX transaction.

In-kind donations: In-kind donations are generally non cash donations such as gifts, services or direct payment of a bill to a 3rd party by the donor. If you do not know the value of your donation we will calculate it for you.

Please make all checks and money orders payable to "ewastegang.org" PO BOX ?? Norristown, PA 19403
